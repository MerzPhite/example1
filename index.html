About me

Ice, Lead Sports Therapist & Director.

I’m Ice, the Lead Sports Therapist and Director of Resolve Sports Therapy. After graduating from the University of Winchester with a BSc in Sports Therapy, I set out to build a clinic that treats every client like an athlete, regardless of their background.

Experience across professional sports therapy and osteopathy clinics helped me craft a hands-on, bespoke approach that prioritises individualised plans over cookie-cutter treatment.

Alongside running Resolve, I am the lead therapist at Procision Football Academy and support runners at events such as the Reading Half Marathon, handling everything from pre-event prep to race-day care.

My mission is simple: change the perception that Sports Therapy is only for elite athletes. Everyone deserves to move better, feel stronger and live without nagging pain.

Outside the clinic, you’ll find me training, playing football or golf, hopping on a paddle board, or firing up the BBQ with family and friends. My passion for rehab began with my own academy football injuries, where I learned firsthand how powerful tailored rehab can be.

The Resolve Method

A clear journey from pain to performance.

Every plan follows a simple structure so you always know what happens next and why. Expect honest conversations, measurable milestones and accountability all the way through.

Phase 1 - Assess

Detailed history, posture & movement testing, neurological screens and palpation to identify the driver of your pain.

Phase 2 - Reset

Hands-on therapy combined with mobility drills and breath work to calm symptoms while restoring range of motion.

Phase 3 - Strengthen

Tailored loading plans, progressive foundations and technique coaching so you re-enter sport feeling stable and confident.

Privacy policy

Your information, your control.

Who we are

Resolve Sports Therapy is a private clinic helping everyone from athletes to office workers regain movement and improve day-to-day quality of life.

Who we share your data with

Your data remains confidential between you and your therapist. We comply with GDPR and patient confidentiality guidelines. Information is only shared with another party if you explicitly nominate a trustee.

How long we retain your data

Records stay on our system until you request removal, keeping you in full control. If you are discharged or simply decide you no longer want us to retain your data, we will delete it promptly.

What rights you have over your data

You may withdraw consent for treatment or request data removal at any time. If you return after removal, you will be treated as a new patient and billed at the new patient rate.
